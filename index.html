Who we are

Ron Addidle

Born in Northern Ireland, Ron's strong work ethic has been a foundation throughout his life. With 24 years in the military, he's developed a keen eye for detail, punctuality, and excellent organizational skills. This discipline and dedication directly benefit the quality and reliability of his plumbing work.

With over 15 years in the plumbing industry, Ron has handled everything from minor leaks to spearheading projects at notable venues like DB Breweries and Alliance Meats. He's been involved in school renovations and retirement village constructions. Having worked across regions from Christchurch to South Canterbury, he's now settled in Nelson for the past five years. Although knowledgeable in drainlaying and gas work, he's not currently licensed to perform these specific tasks.

In his free time, Ron loves exploring the Nelson and Tasman areas. An avid hunter and fisher, he enjoys the outdoors and connecting with the natural beauty of our surroundings.
