What we do

Maintenance

Providing comprehensive plumbing maintenance solutions for your home and business. From leak repairs to fixture replacements, we ensure integrity and durability.

  • Leaking pipes, valves, taps, showers, toilet cistern header tanks, hot water cylinders
  • Burst water mains, in-wall pipes, under-house repairs, ceiling space repairs
  • Fixture replacement
  • Boiling vessel repairs (Zip, Rheem, etc.)
  • Replacement of old piping systems (e.g. Dux Quest)

Renovations and New Builds

High quality renovations of kitchens, bathrooms, laundries, and hot water systems and new builds as directed by you.

  • Renovations of all sizes to suit your needs and budget.
  • Fixture replacement
  • Conversion from low pressure hot water to mains pressure systems
  • Ring main installations

Alterations

Expertise in plumbing alterations to enhance and modernize your property, from pump fittings to boiler installations.

  • Fitting of house pumps, booster pumps and circulating pumps
  • Installation of whole house and point of use filter systems
  • Installation of under bench hot water systems
  • Boiler unit installation
  • Exterior hot water cyclinder installation

About us

Ron Addidle

Born in Northern Ireland, Ron's strong work ethic has been a foundation throughout his life. With 24 years in the military, he's developed a keen eye for detail, punctuality, and excellent organizational skills. This discipline and dedication directly benefit the quality and reliability of his plumbing work.

With over 15 years in the plumbing industry, Ron has handled everything from minor leaks to spearheading projects at notable venues like DB Breweries and Alliance Meats. He's been involved in school renovations and retirement village constructions. Having worked across regions from Christchurch to South Canterbury, he's now settled in Nelson for the past five years. Although knowledgeable in drainlaying and gas work, he's not currently licensed to perform these specific tasks.

In his free time, Ron loves exploring the Nelson and Tasman areas. An avid hunter and fisher, he enjoys the outdoors and connecting with the natural beauty of his surroundings.
